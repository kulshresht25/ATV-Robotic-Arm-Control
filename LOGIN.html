About Prixx Robotics

Founded in 2016, Prixx Robotics is a passionate student-led team dedicated to innovation in robotics. With over 50 members across mechanical, electronics, computer science, and management disciplines, we focus on developing advanced robots for competitions and real-world applications. Our mission is to push boundaries in design, engineering, and teamwork while inspiring the next generation of roboticists.

Project: ATV with Robotic Arm

Overview

The ATV with Robotic Arm is designed to operate in environments that are unsafe, inaccessible, or challenging for humans. Combining all-terrain mobility with a versatile robotic arm, it can navigate rough landscapes and perform precise manipulation tasks remotely.

Real-World Applications

  • Disaster Response: Clear debris or assist in rescue operations without risking human lives.
  • Industrial Safety: Handle hazardous substances in chemical plants, power stations, or nuclear facilities.
  • Military & Defense: Perform reconnaissance, bomb disposal, or supply delivery in high-risk zones.
  • Agriculture & Mining: Access difficult terrains to carry tools or manipulate materials in the field.
  • Space & Research: Modular robotics concepts for planetary exploration and field research.

Future Scope

  • Autonomy: AI-driven navigation for semi-autonomous missions.
  • Vision-Guided Manipulation: Advanced computer vision for automated handling.
  • Scalability: Smaller and larger variants for diverse industries.
  • Connectivity: 5G/LTE-based long-range remote operation.
  • Sustainability: Solar-powered charging for extended field operations.
